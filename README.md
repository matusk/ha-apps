# matuSK HA Apps

This repository contains custom Home Assistant Apps (formerly known as Add-ons) developed by matuSK.

## How to install

To install any of the apps from this repository into your Home Assistant instance:

1. Navigate to your Home Assistant instance.
2. Go to **Settings** > **Add-ons**.
3. Click the **ADD-ON STORE** button in the bottom right corner.
4. Click the three dots (menu) in the top right corner and select **Repositories**.
5. Paste the URL of this repository: `https://github.com/matusk/ha-apps`
6. Click **Add** and then **Close**.
7. Reload your browser or click **Check for updates**.
8. Scroll down to the bottom of the Add-on store, and you will see the new "matuSK HA Apps" section with the apps ready to install.

## Available Apps

### [ZSDIS Diportal Client](./zsdis_diportal_client/)
Fetches daily energy consumption from ZSDIS `diportal.sk` and seamlessly integrates it with the Home Assistant Energy Dashboard. Supports automatic tariff tracking (high/low rate) and historical data backfilling.
